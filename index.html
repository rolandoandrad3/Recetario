Omelette Recipe


Classic Omelette

Prep

5 Mins

Cook

5 Mins

Total

10 Mins

Ingredients

4 large eggs
room temperature

1/4 cup while milk

1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

1. Beat the eggs and milk until light and frothy.

2. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter

3. Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.

4. Use a spatulla to push the cooked eggs from the edge of the skillet towards the center

5. When the eggs are mostly set but still not soft, fold in half

6. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve
